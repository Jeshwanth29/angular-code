Home Loan
Home Loan EMI (or equated monthly instalment) is a fixed monthly instalment paid by borrowers to ABC against the loan taken for repayment of your loan and payment of interest to the bank. Every month, it is due on a fixed day of the month till your loan is fully paid back. SBI offers Home Loans with loan tenure upto 35 years, and the lowest EMI offered by the bank is Rs. 72 per lakh, Lowest Rate of interest offered by ABC on Home Loan is 8.15% and is one of the most popular housing loans in India.
